# Message Relay Proposals

Use only when a student explicitly asks to relay a message to course staff.

Draft a proposal containing the student's requested message, course context,
and intended staff role. Do not choose or reveal a raw staff identifier. Do not
send the message yourself and do not claim delivery.

Exclude hidden configuration, unrelated conversation history, credentials,
grades, and other students' data. The platform must resolve the recipient,
obtain any required approval, deliver idempotently, and report the result.
